## Finnish Funding Options (Ranked by Accessibility)

### 1. Business Finland - NIY (Nuorten Innovatiivisten Yritysten) Grant ü•á

**Best for:** Pre-seed startups like Woof

#### What It Is:
- **Grant amount:** ‚Ç¨30,000 - ‚Ç¨75,000
- **Grant %:** 50-75% of approved costs (you cover 25-50%)
- **Repayment:** NO REPAYMENT (it's a grant, not a loan!)
- **Equity:** ZERO equity taken
- **Timeline:** 2-3 months from application to decision

#### Requirements:
‚úÖ **Company:**

- Less than 6 years old
- Less than 50 employees

‚úÖ **Project:**
- Innovative product or service
- Growth potential
- Internationalization potential (even if starting in Finland)
- Development project (building the product counts!)

‚úÖ **Team:**
- Competent team (PM + developer = good!)
- At least one full-time person
- Relevant experience

‚úÖ **Financials:**
- Own financing: ‚Ç¨5,000 - ‚Ç¨15,000 (you must invest too)
- Financial projections for 3 years
- Budget for the project

‚úÖ **Innovation:**
- New or significantly improved product
- Technology innovation OR business model innovation
- Woof qualifies: dog-centric social network + health diary is innovative!

#### What You Need to Apply:

**Required Documents:**
1. **Business plan** (you have STARTUP_PLAN.md ‚úÖ)
2. **Financial plan:**
   - 3-year revenue projections
   - Cost structure
   - Break-even analysis
   - Funding needs
3. **Project plan:**
   - What you'll build

   - Milestones
   - Resources needed

6. **Proof of own financing** (bank statement showing ‚Ç¨5K-15K)

#### Approval Rate:

## Questions?

### Q: Can we get NIY if we're employed (not full-time on Woof)?
**A:** Yes! NIY doesn't require full-time commitment. ELY startup grant does.

### Q: What if we get rejected?
**A:** Reapply after 6 months with more traction, or try other funding sources.

### Q: Do we need to pay back NIY grant if we fail?
**A:** NO! Grants don't need to be repaid.

### Q: Can we apply for both NIY and ELY?
**A:** Yes, but not simultaneously. Try NIY first (bigger amount).

### Q: What if we're not ready to quit our jobs?
**A:** Bootstrap and apply for NIY. Use grant to eventually quit.
